# Quick Guide: Recurring Bookings

## What is a Recurring Booking?

Book the same resource multiple times automatically - perfect for regular meetings, weekly study groups, or recurring events.

**Example:** Need a conference room every Monday night for your project team? Create one recurring booking instead of 12 individual ones!

## Recurring Patterns Explained

### Weekly
- **What:** Same day & time every week
- **Example:** Every Monday 7-9 PM
- **Use case:** Weekly team meetings, study groups

### Bi-weekly
- **What:** Same day & time every 2 weeks
- **Example:** Every other Wednesday 2-4 PM
- **Use case:** Bi-weekly seminars, alternating meetings

### Daily (Weekdays)
- **What:** Monday through Friday, same time
- **Example:** Mon-Fri 9-10 AM
- **Use case:** Daily stand-ups, practice sessions

### Monthly
- **What:** Same date each month, same time
- **Example:** 15th of every month, 3-5 PM
- **Use case:** Monthly reviews, scheduled maintenance

## Common Questions

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Typically 1-2 business days. Check your email and dashboard.

**Q: Can I cancel one date in the series?**
A: Yes! Each booking can be cancelled individually.

**Q: What if I need to change the time?**
A: Contact admin or cancel and rebook. Future updates may allow bulk editing.

**Q: Can I see all my recurring bookings?**
A: Yes! Go to "My Bookings" and filter by status or search by date.

**Q: What if most dates have conflicts?**
A: System will create what it can. You'll see conflict report. Consider different time/resource.
