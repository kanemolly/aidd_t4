Smart Recommendation Features

  • Book Again - Re-access resources you've used before
  • Location-Based - Discover similar resources at your frequent locations
  • Preference-Matched - Suggestions aligned with your interests
  • Popular Resources - Top-rated items frequently booked by other students
  • One-Click Booking - Direct booking from recommendation cards
  • Responsive Design - Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Smart Hiding - Shows only on fresh page load, hides with search filters
  • Personalized Reasons - Explains why each resource is recommended

Recommendation Strategies

Strategy 1: Book Again

Resources you've previously booked. Quick access to your favorites.

Strategy 2: Same Location

When no preferred locations set, suggests resources at your most frequently visited location.

Strategy 3: Preferred Locations

Uses your saved preferred locations to find matching resources.

Strategy 4: Similar Type

Recommends resources similar to ones you've previously booked.

Strategy 5: Popular Resources

Highly-booked resources as fallback to ensure quality recommendations.
